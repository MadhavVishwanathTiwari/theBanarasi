Banarasi Restaurant

Pure Vegetarian Multi-Cuisine Excellence

Healthy, Hygienic, Authentic

Experience pure vegetarian multi-cuisine dining in our fully air-conditioned restaurant with seating for 100 guests. We serve authentic North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes, crafted with premium ingredients and utmost care.

Our commitment to health and quality means no artificial colors, no ajinomoto, and no palm oil in any of our preparations.

Perfect for family get-togethers, birthdays, and corporate meals, we also offer a small party hall accommodating 20-30 guests for intimate celebrations.

Whether dining with loved ones or hosting business lunches, our comfortable seating, attentive service, and serene ambiance create the perfect setting where every meal is served with warmth, care, and perfection.

Capacity: 100 pax + Party Hall (20-30 pax)
Cuisine: North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, Continental

Banarasi Banquet

Grandeur Meets Elegance - Where Celebrations Come to Life

Lavish Celebrations in the Heart of Gandhidham

Our sprawling banquet area, located at the heart of the city, features majestic heights and dimensions designed to facilitate grandeur for every occasion. From intimate gatherings to lavish weddings accommodating up to 1200 guests, we create unforgettable experiences.

We offer complete event planning and decor services with tailor-made packages to suit each celebration. Our multi-cuisine catering serves authentic delicacies, while ample parking and valet services ensure convenience.

Corporate Hall & Additional Facilities

Within our banquet facility, we feature a spacious corporate hall accommodating up to 150 guests, perfect for training sessions, corporate get-togethers, birthday parties, kitty parties, and karaoke events. Equipped with TV/projector screens and professional sound systems, it's ideal for productive meetings and memorable celebrations.

The venue is fully air-conditioned with 7 AC guest rooms (double bed, 2 guests per room) available for your comfort, complete in-house catering services, and a pure vegetarian, family-friendly environment.

Main Hall: Up to 1200 guests
Corporate Hall: Up to 150 guests with TV/Projector & sound system
Location: Heart of Gandhidham-Kutch
Services: Event planning, decor, multi-cuisine catering
Facilities: Fully AC, parking, valet, 7 AC rooms
Environment: Pure vegetarian, family-friendly
