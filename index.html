Banarasi Restaurant

Authentic Vegetarian Dining Experience

Multicuisine Excellence

At Banarasi Multicuisine Restaurant, we bring you an authentic vegetarian dining experience inspired by India's rich culinary traditions. We serve a wide variety of North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, and Continental dishes, prepared with premium ingredients, aromatic spices, and utmost hygiene.

Our chefs take pride in crafting meals that balance traditional flavors with modern presentation, offering both à la carte and thali options. The restaurant's comfortable seating, attentive service, and serene ambiance make it a perfect spot for family dinners, business lunches, or casual outings.

Every meal and every moment is served with warmth, care, and perfection. Whether you're dining with loved ones or celebrating life's milestones, we promise an experience that's as fulfilling as it is memorable.

North Indian: Authentic flavors from the heart of India
South Indian: Traditional Dosa, Idli & more
Chinese & Continental: International vegetarian favorites
Service: À la carte & thali options available
